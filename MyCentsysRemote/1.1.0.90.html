Release version: 1.1.0.90

Release date: May 31, 2024

Details

Improvements

  • Enhanced App Settings: We've addressed an issue where the widget text-to-speech function would automatically activate upon opening the app settings. You can now control this feature manually for a more personalised experience.
  • Huawei App Optimisation: For our Huawei users, we've removed unsupported Google services from the MyCentsys Remote app on your devices.
  • Shared Device Access: A previous bug that prevented triggering on a shared SMART device on the first attempt on iOS has been resolved. You can now seamlessly activate your CENTURION devices directly from the MyCentsys Remote app.
